# Warmth Brand Identity

> **Visual & Emotional Aesthetic**
> Warmth looks like a quiet room, a warm light, a cozy blanket. It is not a tech tool.

## 1. Colors

**Primary Palette (Warm & Soft):**

- **Peach**: Soft, welcoming warmth.
- **Soft Lavender**: Calming, spiritual.
- **Pastel Blue**: Serenity, sky.
- **Beige**: Grounded, natural.
- **Warm Brown**: Earthy, stable.
- **Soft Grey**: Neutral, balanced.

**Avoid:**

- Bright Red (Alert/Danger)
- Neon Green (Tech/Artificial)
- Aggressive Black (Too dark/stark)

## 2. Typography

**Combination:**

- **Headings (Emotional Text):** `Cormorant Garamond`
  - _Why?_ Elegant, human, expressive, literary.
- **Body/UI (Chat Text):** `Nunito`
  - _Why?_ Rounded, friendly, modern, readable.

**Vibe:**

- Warmth, softness, professionalism, emotional connection.

## 3. Logo Concept

**Symbol:**

- Small flame, heart spark, warm circle, or abstract hug.
- _Feeling:_ "Safe space, warm moment."

## 4. Micro-animations

**Style:**

- Subtle, organic, slow.
- **Typing Dots**: Slow, rhythmic pulse.
- **App Open**: Soft pulse or gentle bloom.
- **Messages**: Gentle fade-in (no abrupt popping).

## 5. Emotional Aesthetic

- **Atmosphere**: Quiet, intimate, safe.
- **Visuals**: Soft gradients, rounded corners, ample whitespace.
- **Metaphor**: A sanctuary, not a dashboard.
